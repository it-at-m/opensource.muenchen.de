## About the project

The sds-calculator repository is a small, static web application for calculating the Digital Sovereignty Score (SDS) based on a short checklist with yes/no answers.

Its purpose is to provide a simple way to assess the degree of digital sovereignty in a lightweight and easy-to-use format.
