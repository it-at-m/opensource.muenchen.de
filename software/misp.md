The __Open Source Threat Intelligence Platform__ (''MISP'') is used as a plattform for sharing Incicators of Compromises (IOCs) and other relevant information accross all parts of the German Cybersecurity network including the City of Munich.

---

In our organisation this Platform is used to widely detect Threat Actor Activity accross our IT-Infrastructure.
"The MISP" gives us the ability to corellate the shared information within our Security monitoring Infrastructure to detect Security occurrences.
It gives us a powerful tool to enhance the simple IOC findings with valuable Enrichment and Knowledge about Threat Actors.
In the future, we will also share Information to the German Cybersecurity network with the help of this plattform.
