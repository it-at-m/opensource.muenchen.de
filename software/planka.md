**PLANKA** is a collaborative project management tool that utilizes the Kanban methodology to help teams organize and track their work visually.

---

In today's fast-paced work environments, effective project management is crucial for team success. PLANKA offers a user-friendly interface that allows teams to visualize their workflows, prioritize tasks, and collaborate seamlessly.

With features such as customizable boards, drag-and-drop task management, and real-time collaboration, Planka empowers teams to stay on track and deliver results efficiently.

It is container-based and can be deployed easily, making it a scalable solution for organisations of all sizes.
